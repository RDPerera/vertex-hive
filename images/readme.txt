Place these image files in this directory:

1. system.png - Your security system image with a white/transparent background
2. logo.png - Company logo for the navigation bar

The system.png should ideally have:
- Clean white or transparent background
- Good resolution (at least 600px wide)
- Clear view of the product

The logo.png should:
- Be approximately 40px in height
- Have a transparent background if possible
- Be readable at small sizes
